Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Drift ("we", "our", or "the app") is an AI dream journal for iOS. We built Drift with privacy as a core principle. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Drift collects only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Dream entries — the text of dreams you record or type, stored locally on your device using Apple's Swift Data framework.
  • Voice recordings — audio recorded through your microphone is transcribed on-device using Apple's SFSpeechRecognizer. The audio file is never uploaded to any server.
  • Dream text for AI analysis — when you tap "Interpret my dream", the text of your dream (not audio) is sent to Anthropic's API to generate a psychological reflection. See Section 3.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No account or registration is required
  • No name, email address, or personal identifiers
  • No location data
  • No device tracking or advertising identifiers (no IDFA)
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No audio recordings uploaded to any server
  • No data sold to third parties — ever

3. Third-Party Services

Anthropic API — dream text is sent to Anthropic (anthropic.com) solely to generate AI interpretations. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this data: anthropic.com/privacy. Anthropic does not use API inputs to train their models by default. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests.

Apple SFSpeechRecognizer — voice transcription is performed on-device. No audio leaves your iPhone.

Apple StoreKit — subscription purchases are handled entirely by Apple. We do not process or store payment information.

4. Data Storage

All your dream entries, symbols, and pattern data are stored locally on your device using Swift Data. This data is included in your standard iOS device backup (iCloud or iTunes) if you have backups enabled. We do not operate any servers that store your personal data.

5. Data Deletion

Deleting the Drift app from your iPhone permanently removes all locally stored data. Individual dreams can be deleted from within the Journal screen at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

Drift is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
